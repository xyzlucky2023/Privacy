User Agreement / 用户协议

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

With Apple subscription, the system will deduct the corresponding amount from your App Store account when you confirm your purchase.

If you do not turn off automatic renewal at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period, the system will automatically charge you at the beginning of the next subscription period.

After purchase, you can manage your subscription and automatic renewal settings in your account settings.

Management path: Settings >> User Options >> Subscribe, then select the corresponding item to modify or cancel.

生效日期：2026年2月26日

通过 Apple 订阅服务购买时，在您确认购买后，系统将从您的 App Store 账户中扣除相应费用。

若您未在当前订阅周期结束前至少 24 小时关闭自动续费，系统将在下一订阅周期开始时自动扣费。

购买完成后，您可在账户设置中管理订阅及自动续费。

管理路径：设置 >> 用户选项 >> 订阅，选择对应项目进行修改或取消。
